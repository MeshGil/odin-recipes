Indomie Karee

Menu Description

A quick fix for your breakfast, lunch or dinner. Very tasty and simple to make

Type : Instant Food
Price : Very Cheap
Taste : Amazing Indonesian Karee

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Noodle
  • Water
  • Chilli Sauce
  • Instant chicken broth powder
  • Chicken meat
  • Vegetable

Steps

  1. Boil 200cc of Water
  2. Prepare broth powder, chilli and soy sauce in your bowl
  3. Soak noodle in boiled water for 3 minutes
  4. add noodle and some broth into the bowl
  5. add Chicken and vegetable as the topping
  6. Noodle is ready to be served
